This spreadsheet contains all the data on Materials, Effects and Recipes you need to know to fully understand the cooking system in Tears of the Kingdom.

List of Tabs on this Sheet:
6
Steps - Order of operations the game does when cooking a meal (casual friendly)
7
Ingredients master list - A complete and dense list of all 251 ingredients with all their properties regarding cooking
8
Single Recipes - A complete list of all single-material recipes, in the order the game checks for them
9
Normal Recipes - A complete list of all non single-material recipes, in the order the game checks for them
10
Effect data and potencies/levels - A complete list of all used cooking effects with all their properties and level/potency equivalents
11
Monster Extract - A quick resume of how Monster Extract behaves
12
Criticals - A quick resume of how Critical Hits behave (only happens if no Monster Extract is present)
13
Effect ingredients - A list of all ingredients that can give your meal an effect and their properties relative to effects
14
Time ingredients - A list of all effect-less ingredients that can give your meal an added duration (not counting the universal +0:30 from material amount)
15
Health ingredients - A list of all ingredients that can add health recovery to your meal (either by their raw health recovery, or by their Spice)
16
Critical ingredients - A list of all ingredients that can boost the critical rate of your meal
