1Getting the recipeThe game tries to find a recipe that matches the ingredients list provided. If it fails to do so, the result will be the failed meal actor (Dubious Food).There are two lists the game can search through. More details in the Cooking document. If Dubious Food, skip to the end.
3
2Getting the effect and its statsThe game finds the effect of the meal (if it exists) as well as its duration and level.Dubious Food and Rock-Hard Food have no effect, and zero duration and level.
4
2.1EffectIf the amount of different effects provided by the materials is exactly one, the meal takes that effect. Else, the meal has no effect (unless it's an elixir, and then it becomes Dubious Food).-
5
2.2Effect levelThe game adds the potency of each ingredient possessing the effect of the meal, then multiplies the sum of potency with the Rate of the effect and clamps it to the Max level of the effect to get the final level.-
6
2.3Effect durationEach material of the list adds 30 seconds. Each material with the effect of the meal adds the effect base time. Each CookEnemy material (not necessarily unique) addds its Spice time.-
7
3Health recoveryEach material of the list adds its health recovery to the meal. The sum is then multiplied by 2. 4 health = 1 Heart.Dubious Food regenerates 1 Heart, Rock-Hard Food regenerates ¼ Heart, both have 2 selling price and no effect, skip to the end in that case.
8
4Monster ExtractApplies the Monster Extract algorithm (see Monster Extract) if there is a Monster Extract in the materials list.In such a case, skip to step 7.
9
5Critical rateThe critical rate is 5 times the amount of unique materials in the meal, added to the maximum Added critical chance out of all the materials in the meal.-
10
6Critical hitApplies the Critical algorithm (see Criticals) if a critical hit is rolled. Monster Extracts inhibit Criticals.-
11
7Non CookEnemy SpiceEach unique non-CookEnemy material adds its Spice time to the duration and its Spice health recovery to the health recovery.-
12
8Meal bonusesAdds the Bonus health of the meal to the health recovery.-
13
9Adjusting valuesClamp duration to max (30:00), clamp health recovery to max (160), if >= 120 also set to 160, if no effect and no health recovery, set health recovery to ¼ Heart, clamp level to max and min level of the effect, if Extra Heart or Gloom Recovery round level to nearest 4, level is rounded down. Dubious Food regenerates 1 Heart, Rock-Hard Food regenerates ¼ Heart, both have 2 selling price and no effect, skip to the end in that case.
14
10Selling priceEach material adds its selling price to the meal's selling price (or 1 if they're a Cook low price), it then is multiplied with the sell price rate. Fairy Tonic has its selling price set to 2.Sell price rate is 1.2 for one material, 1.3 for two, 1.4 for three, 1.6 for four, 1.8 for five.
